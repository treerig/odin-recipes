Smoothie Recipe

Description

This is a delicous fruit smoothie. It is very quick to make and serves as a nice breakfast or postworkout snack. Use organic fruit for best results.

Ingredients

  • 1 apple
  • 1 orange
  • 1 bannana
  • 6 oz of apple juice
  • 1 strawberry

Steps

  1. peel bannana and orange, discard peels
  2. slice apple and discard core
  3. slice strawberry and set aside for garnish
  4. combine bannana, orange, apple, and apple juice in blender
  5. blend thoroughly
  6. pour in glass
  7. garnish with sliced strawberry
  8. serve and enjoy!
