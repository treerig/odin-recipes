Steak Recipe

Description

This is a hearty meal that will leave you full. Lots of protien and fat. Especially good after a long day in the woods.

Ingredients

  • one steak
  • splash of olive oil
  • knob of butter
  • spice mix: a pinch of salt, pepper

Steps

  1. apply spice mix to steak
  2. heat butter and oil in pan
  3. add steak to hot pan
  4. cook until nice crust forms then flip
  5. cook until nice crust forms, then remove
  6. let rest for 2 min
  7. serve and enjoy!
