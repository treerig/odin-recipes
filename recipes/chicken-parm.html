Chicken Parm Recipe

Description

This is a delicous recipe that is guaranteed to fill you up. It is not too difficult and totally worth the cleanup. The chicken is crispy, with gooey cheese on top. The chicken rests on a bed of spaghetti. Tomato sauce provides a nice level of moisture to the dish. A true classic.

Ingredients

  • two chicken breasts
  • 12 oz tomato sauce
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • a handful of flour
  • 1 lb of pasta
  • 5 oz fresh mozz
  • a hand full of grated parm
  • frying station: 1 egg, handfull of flour, handful of breadcrumbs
  • spice mix: a pinch of salt, pepper, garlic powder

Steps

  1. apply spice mix to chicken
  2. apply frying station to chicken in following order: flour, egg, breadcrumbs
  3. fry chicken in olive oil
  4. slice mozz and put on top of chicken
  5. sprinkle parm on chicken
  6. serve chicken assembly over cooked pasta
  7. apply tomato sauce over chicken and pasta
  8. serve and enjoy!
